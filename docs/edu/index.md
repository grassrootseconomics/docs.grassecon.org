# Education

This section collects learning materials for communities, facilitators, students, researchers, and partners who want to understand Economic Commons, Community Asset Vouchers, Commitment Pools, and regenerative exchange.

## Learning materials

* [Frequently Asked Questions](faq/): Plain-language answers about Commitment Pooling, Community Asset Vouchers, Sarafu Network, and starting a pool.
* [Glossary](glossary/): Core terms used across the documentation.
* [Community Training Guide](training/): Facilitator material for workshops, simulations, and practical learning.
* [Case Study](case_study/): Commitment Pooling in Binguni and Mbele, Kenya.
* [Book](https://willruddick.substack.com/p/grassroots-economics-the-book-is): Long-form public writing on Grassroots Economics.

## Games and public learning

* [Social Soil](https://play.grassecon.org): A farming and village-trading game about ecology, cooperation, local exchange, and community systems.
* [Games on grassecon.org](https://grassecon.org/games): Educational games for classrooms, workshops, and community learning spaces.

## Research and public data

Research, publications, and datasets are listed on [grassecon.org/research](https://grassecon.org/research). Sarafu Network activity and reports are available through [sarafu.network](https://sarafu.network).
